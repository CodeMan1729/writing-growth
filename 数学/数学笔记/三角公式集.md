# 重要的三角函数相关极限

## 极限1
>[!tips] 
>$$\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{sinx}{x}=1$$

### 题目1
>[!note] 推广的-李杨数学分析强化讲义上册-例题1.1.2 
>$$f(\theta, n)= \prod_{k=1}^{n} \cos\!\left(\frac{\theta}{2^k}\right)= \cos\frac{\theta}{2}\cdot\cos\frac{\theta}{2^2}\cdot\cos\frac{\theta}{2^3}\cdots\cos\frac{\theta}{2^n}.$$
>求$$\lim_{(\theta,n)\to(0,+\infty)}f(\theta, n)$$

😘，根据[[三角公式集#等式1]]
## 等式1
>[!tips] 
>$$\cos\frac{\theta}{2}\cdot\cos\frac{\theta}{2^2}\cdot\cos\frac{\theta}{2^3}\cdots\cos\frac{\theta}{2^n}=\frac{sin\theta}{2^nsin(\frac{\theta}{2^n})}$$
